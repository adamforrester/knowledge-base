Design tokens
Design tokens represent small, repeatable design
decisions. A token can be a color, font style, unit of
white space, or even a motion animation designed
for a specific need.

Why:
• Delivers visual consistency across digital products.
• Enables themes (dark mode, different brands).
• Simplifies design and development by streamlining
  handoff.
• As Thrive’s visual language evolves, changes can
  be made once across the system and products. No
  more finding and replacing hard-coded values
  everywhere.

FRESH
DESIGN SYSTEM
OPPORTUNITY
Inconsistent and inaccessible digital experiences were leading to
frustration for Wendy’s users. These issues stemmed from a lack of
standardization across it’s products. In addition to a poor experience, the
fragmentation meant updates and expansions were unnecessarily time-
consuming and resource-intensive, translating to a slow time-to-market
and increased costs.

SOLUTION
Wendy’s Fresh Design System was created to provide an accessible, reusable
standard to support a rebuild of the existing mobile iOS & Android apps into a
single code-base in Flutter. In doing so it has allowed a reduction of 4000 lines
of code in the app, and helped increase time-to-market speeds by 600%. It has
since expanded to support Wendy’s digital ordering website as well as the CRM
platform. It also provides usage guidelines as a reference for best practices.

In the digital food ordering sphere, there is no clear standout when it comes
to accessibility, especially for screen reader users. Their experiences have
largely been unaccounted for. Wendy's has the potential to be the most
accessible food ordering app ever, and be a gold star standard for screen
reader experiences when ordering tasty nuggs.


SOLUTION
From logging current issues, to remediation, to baking accessibility into the
entirety of our process, the Wendy's digital team has been tackling accessibility
head-on. The Design System is a shining example of how great Accessibility
can look.
