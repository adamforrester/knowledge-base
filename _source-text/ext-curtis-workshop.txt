Planning / Major Release / Tracks



Whether for a first release or a next generation, releasing a catalog-wide major release often requires:


Design Language                            A language of color, typography, space and more applied to reference designs


Technical Architecture                     Environments, hosting, and approach to code, test, package, release, and version features


Visual Style                               Color, typography, grid, space and other visual properties, often as design tokens


UI Components                              Design, code and documentation of a UI component catalog


Documentation Platform                     Information architecture, content management, and page templates to publish online documentation


Planning                                   Scoping, alignment, and coordination of work through a major release


Core Team Operations                       Definition and adjustment to a teams management, staff, and ways of working


Adoption Enablement                        Coordination with implementing teams to adopt the system, including early pilots
