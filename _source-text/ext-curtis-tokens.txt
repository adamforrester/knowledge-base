About tokens / Level types

          Level                    use when                                                          examples                        Generic   Semantic   C o m p.

       system                      Defining a top level namespace.                                   slds, mds

         Theme                     Naming a theme, such as color palette or product line.            seafoam,courtyard

       Domain                      Enabling a business unit to customize a reusable set.             consumer, marketing

         Group                     Organizing tokens relevant to many related components.            forms, layered

   Component                       Applying a token to a specific component.                         button, icon, card

      Element                      Applying a token to an element of a specific component.           title, action, image

    c at e g o ry                  Organizing tokens into a prototypical category.                   color, font, space

     concept                       Distinguishing sets of tokens into familiar conceptual groups.    feedback, heading

     property                      Aligning a token with an object’s property.                       background, size, padding

      va r i a n t                 Enumerating a set of alternatives.                                red vs blue, error vs success

          s tat e                  Enumerating alternative states of an object.                      default, hover, disabled

         scale                     Enumerating stops on a potentially continuous / infinite scale.   16, 2x, half-x, 300

           mode                    Toggling display based on a mode, usually light vs dark.          light, dark
