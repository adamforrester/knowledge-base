This is VML.

human connection.
Who We Are
VML is a global creative company that builds connected brands to drive growth. We
partner with organizations around the world to create experiences that are impactful
and deeply true to each brand’s identity. Our integrated capabilities across strategy,
experience design, technology, and creativity allow us to bring brand ambition and
real human insight together in meaningful ways.

We understand the balance required to deliver inspiring brand experiences while
ensuring clarity, consistency, and ease across every interaction. Our work helps
brands show up with purpose, build lasting connections, and create experiences
people genuinely value.


We Are Human First
In a world obsessed with speed, scale, and AI, we choose to start with people. Being
Human First means designing with empathy, grounding decisions in real user needs,
and ensuring technology enhances rather than replaces emotional connection. We
prioritize clarity over complexity, intention over automation, and experiences that
make people feel understood from the moment they arrive.


Our Mission is Simple
Our goal is to create experiences that begin with people, turning real human needs
and emotions into meaningful brand moments that build trust, loyalty, and long-term
growth.

FRESH
DESIGN SYSTEM
OPPORTUNITY
Inconsistent and inaccessible digital experiences were leading to
frustration for Wendy’s users. These issues stemmed from a lack of
standardization across it’s products. In addition to a poor experience, the
fragmentation meant updates and expansions were unnecessarily time-
consuming and resource-intensive, translating to a slow time-to-market
and increased costs.

SOLUTION
Wendy’s Fresh Design System was created to provide an accessible, reusable
standard to support a rebuild of the existing mobile iOS & Android apps into a
single code-base in Flutter. In doing so it has allowed a reduction of 4000 lines
of code in the app, and helped increase time-to-market speeds by 600%. It has
since expanded to support Wendy’s digital ordering website as well as the CRM
platform. It also provides usage guidelines as a reference for best practices.
