     _carbondesign
                            When I started investigating how AI tools could
     Donnie D'Amato
                            leverage all this metadata, I stumbled into the world
     EightShapes

     Salesforce Designer
                            servers).
     Dan Brown

     Karissa Woodward-H…
                            An MCP connects your metadata-rich assets to your
                            AI solutions. Instead of prompting ChatGPT or
                            Claude with isolated questions and limited context,
                            an MCP server is an environment that lets you feed
                            it structured, embedded knowledge for components,
                            icons, tokens, and behavioral rules, all tied to your
                            system’s context through API integrations with your
                            design and development tools.


                            Product teams are already experimenting with
                            Cursor: They’re using MCP to let AI understand
     Home                   components in code, suggest documentation, and
     Library                even prototype full UIs. Now imagine combining
                            that with Figma metadata and real design usage
     Profile
                            data. That’s where we’re headed.
